About Sam Bryan

Biography

My name is Samantha Bryan, but I much prefer to go by Sam. I am a student at Stevens Institute of Technology, where I am pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I am going to be a senior this fall. While at Stevens, I joined the Co-Op program where I did 3 Co-Ops. I am currently working on my third Co-Op this summer at Hudson Bay Capital Management as a Software Developer. I also worked on the same team there last fall for my second Co-Op. While the Co-Op program is typically a 5 year program, I am on track to graduate in 4 years. Through hard work and schedule management, I have been able to complete my degree in 4 years by taking classes over the summer, such as this one, as well as over winter study abroad terms. When I graduate I hope that I will be able to continue working at Hudson Bay Capital as a full-time Software Developer.

Outside of school and work, I have always been a very active person. I have played golf and soccer since I could walk. Both of which I continued to play through high school and college. During my junior year of high school, I started to play basketball after being recruited by the coach because of my height and ahtleticism. After going to college, I decided that I wanted to try volleyball, which I never could in high school because it was the same season as soccer. I joined the club team and ended up being quite good and becoming secretary for two years. When I am home I enjoy playing pickleball a lot, and have joined a league and made some great freinds through that. I hope that I can continue my active lifestyle all through college and into my adult life.
